How to predict site purchases without using ML algorithms?

Every non-targeted e-commerce campaign wastes money. The real key lies in understanding purchase refusal and temporal friction.

I ignored abstractions and fully leaned into the mathematical engine of NumPy in Python to isolate crucial customer patterns. By analyzing logs, I built a behavioral matrix and successfully validated a robust $252 average ticket.

Statistically, the 0.83 temporal correlation effectively demonstrates who is actually ready to buy, targeting the exact right audience with remarketing instead of paying for empty clicks.
